About this project.

This project was inspired and powered by the Women Who Code (https://www.womenwhocode.com) 5th Austin Diversity Hackathon (2019) and Open Austin (https://www.open-austin.org/projects/"). As a volunteer financial coach for Prosper Centers, a part of Foundation Communities (https://foundcom.org) in Austin, TX, I want to connect clients with more and better financial resources and information to plan and make the best possible decisions, to empower themselves and their families.

Currently, this application allows clients interested in affordable housing programs in the Austin area to estimate their maximum rental cost for properties in different areas of Austin. We look forward to expanding this project to include other information clients find helpful in making better financial decisions. Please contact alexandra@alexandrahubbard.com for information you'd like to see on this app and/or add some your ideas and join the project here: https://github.com/social-housing/affordable-housing-atx.

Special thanks to the original Hackathon team that brought this idea to life: Stephanie Caulley, Steven De Avila, Charisse Giles, Devin Dias and Alexandra Hubbard.
